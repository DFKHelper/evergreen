**Detailed Information on CFT:**

**What is CFT?**
- Very gentle, natural approach
- Theory: Craniosacral and Fascial systems are one interconnected system

**What is Fascia?**
- Body's connective tissue
- Framework supporting muscles, organs, entire body
- Protects and envelopes organs, muscles, nerves, blood

**How is Fascia Damaged?**
- Difficult birth
- Surgery
- Auto accidents
